# D1-KP-03: Risk Analytics Methodology and Limitations

Status: review requested. Deadlines: draft T1+6h; review T1+8h.

Research records identify a methodology reference, required inputs, evidence, assumptions, limitations, and artifact links. Supported Day 1 analytical topics include simple and log returns, annualized volatility, maximum drawdown, historical value at risk, historical expected shortfall, deterministic scenario shocks, and contribution summaries.

Presentation must retain horizon, confidence level where applicable, sample period, observation count, methodology, warnings, and evidence references. Tail measures require an inadequate-sample warning when relevant. A missing or incomplete input cannot be interpreted as zero.

The planning package catalogues methods; it does not calculate analytics. No catalogue item is investment advice, an optimization, hedge, trade, rebalance, or order recommendation.
